### Visual identity
- Theme: "Carbon" — near-black surfaces, vivid yellow-amber accent

- surface-raised: #1E1E1E
- surface-card: #242424
- ink-primary: #F0F0F0
- ink-secondary: #8A8A8A
- accent: #C8960A (goldenrod; darker yellow — used for highlights, active states, bullseye 11 button)
- accent-on-accent: #141414 (text on accent background)
- border: #2A2A2A
- confirmed-target: #2E7D32 (green — target complete)
- confirmed-target-text: #A5D6A7
- Style: minimal, sleek, no images, large thumb-friendly tap targets (≥56px for primary actions)

### Layout & interaction decisions
- Typography: Roboto (Quasar/Material default) — clean, legible at all sizes; monospace for score numbers
- Corner rounding: Medium — 8px for inputs/rows, 12px for cards and panels
- Shell landing: 2-column app icon grid — scales to many apps, compact
- Shot button layout: 3+2 grid — bottom row: [0] [5] [8] (most common); top row: [10] [11] (rare, deliberate tap required)
- 11 button: accent (#E8B400) filled, black label — visually distinct bullseye indicator
- Archery home: full-width "New Session" primary button (accent), "History" as a secondary list entry below
- Confirmed target icon: green (#2E7D32 fill); unconfirmed: surface-card (#242424) with border
- Score entry panel: slides up from bottom (bottom sheet pattern) over the scoring board
- Resume/discard in-progress session: bottom sheet over the archery home screen (not full-screen takeover)
- Overall philosophy: keep it simple — minimal chrome, clear hierarchy, no decoration for its own sake
- Icons: monochromatic SVG only — no emojis, no colorful icons anywhere
